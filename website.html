Meet Jerry

The dog is a mammal with sharp teeth, an excellent sense of smell,
and a fine sense of hearing. Each of its four legs ends in a foot,
or paw, with five toes. Each toe has a soft pad and a claw.
A coat of hair keeps the dog warm.

Meet Tom

Cats have sharp, retractable (except in the cheetah)
claws, and their teeth are adapted for stabbing, anchoring, and cutting.
They almost always land on their feet when they fall from a height.
Most species are nocturnal, and their eyes are adapted for seeing in low light.
